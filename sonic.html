Sonic official Lore (sonic 1-sonic adventure 2)



Sonic 1

Dr.robotnik(aka Dr.Eggman) capture animal in robots called badniks on the south island. Sonic see it and decide to stop him he adventure from place to place collect chaos emerald along the way and met eggman in scrap brain zone. Eggman is defeated.

Sonic 2

Sonic and Tails work together to defeat Dr. Eggman, who attacks West Side Island. The destroyed space fortress, the Death Egg, crashes and crashes into Angel Island.

Sonic CD

Sonic Rescues Amy, who was Kidnapped by Metal Sonic. Amy, who had a fateful encounter with Sonic on Little Planet, became embroiled in Dr. Eggman's evil deeds and was kidnapped by Metal Sonic. Sonic defeated Metal Sonic and rescued Amy, thus crushing Dr. Eggman's ambitions.

Sonic 3 and Knuckles

Master Emerald Recaptured & Death Egg Revival Thwarted. Dr. Eggman tricks Knuckles into stealing the Master Emerald and plots to revive the crashed space fortress, the Death Egg. However, Sonic, Tails, and Knuckles work together to destroy the Death Egg, thwarting Dr. Eggman's ambitions of world domination. The Master Emerald is restored, and Angel Island rises into the sky once again.

Sonic Mania

The Mysterious secret stone "Phantom Ruby" (Sonic Mania) - Dr. Eggman unearths a gem with mysterious powers called the "Phantom Ruby," which sends Sonic, Tails, and Knuckles to various worlds. After being defeated by Sonic and his friends, the Phantom Ruby leaves Dr. Eggman's hands and disappears across time and space into the future (Sonic Forces).

Sonic adventure 1

Dr. Eggman releases Chaos, who was sealed away in the Master Emerald, and tries to use it to take over the world. However, Chaos goes out of control and evolves into "Perfect Chaos", bringing the world to the brink of great destruction. However, thanks to Sonic and the will of many people, the Chaos Emeralds, which had lost their power, are restored, and Sonic transforms into Super Sonic. He stops Perfect Chaos and crushes Dr. Eggman's ambitions.

Sonic adventure 2

Dr. Eggman awakens Shadow and tries to use him to take over the world, but Shadow uses him instead to carry out the revenge plan left by Gerald. In the final stage of his revenge, Shadow regains his true memories thanks to Amy. He cooperates with Sonic and saves the world at the cost of his own life.
